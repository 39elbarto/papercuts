## Residual risks and stop conditions

- Private storage is not encryption; accepted text is not redacted.
- Scanner misses documented encodings, entropy-only values, Unicode
  normalization/homoglyphs, and unknown formats.
- Historical journal bytes retain legacy paths and unscanned text.
- Pilot must stop on disclosure, unhealthy doctor, contract drift, ambiguous
  binary identity, or any need for autonomous override.
- Multi-project inventory/digest and general distribution remain blocked.
